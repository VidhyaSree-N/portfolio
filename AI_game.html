HR Compliance Investigation: Anti-Harassment Training

This project is an interactive compliance training game designed to replace traditional HR quizzes with scenario-based gameplay. Players take on the role of a detective investigating workplace harassment in a realistic 3D office environment.

Gameplay & Features:

  • AI-powered NPC interactions based on collected evidence
  • Real-time AI hints and learning notes to guide players
  • Immersive 3D experience built using Three.js and WebGL
  • Evidence collection from CCTV, emails, and system logs
  • AI-generated investigation reports and compliance feedback
  • Embedded HR guidebook covering workplace harassment policies

The game dynamically adapts to player decisions and evaluates their understanding of anti-harassment policies in real time.

Next Steps: Future versions will include multiplayer investigations, improved AI models, and possibly VR/AR training integrations for immersive learning.
