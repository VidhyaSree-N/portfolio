OSDMeasure & OSDGrid Plugins for Axolotl Research

Our tools, OSDMeasure and OSDGrid, are designed to assist with axolotl research, providing precise measurements and customizable grids for accurate data collection. OSDMeasure allows researchers to measure distances between points on zoomable images, while OSDGrid adds a customizable grid overlay for detailed analysis.

These plugins were created to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of measurements in axolotl studies. With features like real-time measurements, export functionality, and multi-grid support, researchers can achieve more precise results.

The tools are built to work seamlessly with OpenSeaDragon and provide an intuitive user interface for easy use in both field and laboratory settings. Whether tracking physical characteristics or spatial data, these tools support the comprehensive analysis required in axolotl research.
